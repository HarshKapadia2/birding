# Rio Grande Valley Birding

## Legend

- WBC: [World Birding Center](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Birding_Center) (a collection of birding sites)
- SP: State Park
- NWR: National Wildlife Refuge

## Itinerary

### Day 0

Reach McAllen at night

        - [Bentsen-Rio Grande Val. SP--Hawk Watch](https://ebird.org/hotspot/L613081)
- Quinta Mazatlan (WBC)
    - [Site](https://quintamazatlan.com)

- Lunch

    - Spots
        - [Santa Ana NWR--Entry and Parking lot](https://ebird.org/hotspot/L1331912)

        - [Santa Ana NWR--Willow Lakes](https://ebird.org/hotspot/L1322059)
        - [Santa Ana NWR--Pintail Lakes Trail](https://ebird.org/hotspot/L1487217)
        - [Santa Ana NWR--Observation Tower](https://ebird.org/hotspot/L1154119)
        - [Santa Ana NWR--Tour Road](https://ebird.org/hotspot/L1322051)
            - Probably for the Wildlife Drive
        - [Santa Ana NWR--Cattail Lake](https://ebird.org/hotspot/L1189369)
- Dinner

Sleep in McAllen
