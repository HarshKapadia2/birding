# Rio Grande Valley Birding

Last updated: 27th April 2026

## Introduction

- One of the best places to bird in the USA, especially for migrants in the spring and fall.
- Carrying **mosquito/bug repellent**, sunscreen and water is very important.
- The itinerary is quite packed and geared towards birding only, but feel free to modify it.
    - During our first trip in April 2026, Hugh Ramsey Park and Resaca de la Palma didn't have a lot of birds, so we added two more birding spots.
- Details for each spot are below the itinerary.
- If you wish, follow Rare Bird Alerts (from eBird) for Hidalgo and Cameron counties.
    - We consciously decided to not do this for our first trip in April 2026.

## Past Trips

- [Rio Grande Valley and South Padre Island, TX, USA (Apr 2026)](https://ebird.org/tripreport/506981)

## Legend

- RGV: Rio Grande Valley
- WBC: [World Birding Center](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Birding_Center) (a collection of birding sites)
- SP: State Park
- NWR: National Wildlife Refuge

## Itinerary

### Day 0

- Leave Austin in the evening and reach McAllen at night. (6 hrs)

### Day 1

- Drive (20 mins) from McAllen
- Bentsen-Rio Grande Valley State Park (WBC) (3.5 hrs)
- Drive (20 mins)
- Quinta Mazatlan (WBC) (2 hrs)
- Drive + lunch (20 mins + 15 mins for pickup)
- Edinburg Scenic Wetlands and World Birding Center (WBC) (2 hrs)
- Drive (30 mins)
- Santa Ana NWR (3.5 hrs)
- Drive (30 mins)
- Dinner at [Ana Liz Taqueria](https://maps.app.goo.gl/nuVpMUEQUL4x22zt6) (4-10 PM CT) (1.5 hrs)
- Drive (15 mins)
- Sleep in McAllen

### Day 2

- Leave McAllen
- Drive (30 mins)
- Estero Llano Grande State Park (WBC) (3.5 hrs)
- Drive (30 mins)
- Hugh Ramsey Park (Harlingen Arroyo Colorado) (WBC) (1.5 hr)
- Drive + lunch (30 mins + 15 mins for pickup)
- Resaca de la Palma State Park (WBC) (2 hrs)
- Drive (30 mins)
- Sabal Palm Sanctuary (2 hrs) (USA border gate closes at 5 PM CT, so need to leave outermost sanctuary gate by 4:45 PM CT)
- Drive (20 mins)
- Sleep in Brownsville

### Day 3

- Leave Brownsville
- Drive (10 mins)
- UTRGV Brownsville (1 hr)
- Drive (1 hr)
- South Padre Island (WBC) (9 hrs)
- Sea Turtle, Inc.
- Beach
- Return to Austin (7 hrs)
